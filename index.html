To get started you will need a raspberry pi if you don't have one already. They are available from a variety of suppliers including buyapi.ca. Our base SD card is the perfect product to accompany your first Raspberry Pi thanks to it's hassle free approach to getting you sert up for coding.

All of our Micro SD cards come with Raspbian OS pre-installed. Our best selling product is our web automation optomized Micro SD card. This card comes with two python scripts already available; one base and another more complex web automation program. These programs are designed to allow for easy customization using python. This allows for more flexibility when it comes to meeting for your unique web automation needs. All of this is possible thanks to Selenium and Chromium Web Driver packages that our devellopers have preinstalled for easy access on our web automation SD card.

First time Pi owners

Our base Micro SD card is the simplest way to get your Raspberry pi up and running. If you are a first time Pi owner and are intimidated by the complex instructions for setting up your first Pi you don't need to worry any more! Simply purchase one of our Micro SD cards and wait for us to mail it to your doorstep. Once you receive the card, simply slide it into your Raspberry Pi's Micro SD card slot. Now plug in your Pi and you are ready to go!

Students participating in asynchronous learning

During the current pandemic people are spending more time learning online. Some online classes are run in an asynchronous fashion with work being assigned for days at home. While this process within itself is fine, teachers are still INEXPLICABLY asking students to fill out attendance each morning before class. This means that even though work is assigned for an entire day, students are still required to wake up by a certain time. Our web automation SD cards are set up to allow the user to customize them to automatically fill out virtual attendance each morning. Our modern solution offers students peace of mind, and a good nights sleep, to allow for more productive at home school days.

How to get automation programs up and running

  1. Get a raspberry pi with a wifi conncetion
  2. Purchase one of our preformated sd cards
  3. Boot your Pi from our SD card
  4. Try out our preinstalled programs (Further instructions are included as a text document with each product)
  5. By writing your own python programs or by customizing the pre-existing programs, you can write web automation scripts
